ABOUT DLSJBC

Developing Future Lasallian Leaders

De La Salle John Bosco College is dedicated to providing quality Catholic education rooted in the Lasallian tradition of faith, service, and communion. The institution promotes academic excellence, leadership, innovation, and social responsibility, preparing students to become competent professionals and compassionate citizens.

OUR HISTORY

A Legacy of Excellence

From its humble beginnings, De La Salle John Bosco College has continued to grow as one of the leading educational institutions in the region. Through decades of dedication to quality education, the school has remained committed to forming students who excel academically while embodying Christian values. Today, DLSJBC continues to embrace innovation, community engagement, and global competitiveness, ensuring every graduate is prepared to serve society.

OUR FOUNDATION

Vision, Mission & Core Values

Vision

To be a leading Catholic Lasallian educational institution recognized for academic excellence, Christian formation, innovation, and service to society while developing globally competitive graduates.

Mission

We provide quality Lasallian education that nurtures faith, develops competence, promotes social responsibility, and inspires lifelong learning through excellent teaching, research, and community engagement.
