Questions About Our Academic Programs

What academic programs does DLSJBC offer?

How can I apply for admission?

Complete the admission requirements, submit the necessary documents, and follow the enrollment procedures provided by the school.

Scholarship opportunities may be available depending on school policies and partner organizations. Contact the Admissions Office for more information.
